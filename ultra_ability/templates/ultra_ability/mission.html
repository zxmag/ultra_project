Mission

Ultra-Ability Ministry aims to meet both the spiritual and physical needs of people, by helping them develop and maintain a sacred love relationship with our Creator through free in-depth Bible teaching, bringing healing through loving chaplaincy The Chapel of Compassion, and providing physical relief to families in developing countries through the Unite in Love campaign.

Vision

As a vessel of God, Ultra-Ability Ministry hopes to empower individuals and families with the loyal love and tender mercy of God so that they'll live with ultra-ability through the divine power despite life's storms.

Values

Ultra-Ability Ministry serves people with integrity, and promotes hope, humor and healing.
