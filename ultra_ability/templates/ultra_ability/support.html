If you would like to support Ultra-Ability Ministry, you may do so in two ways: spiritually through your prayers, and financially through your donations or the purchase of any of Shirley Cheng's Books.

PRAYERS

"I exhort therefore, first of all, that petitions, prayers, intercessions, and givings of thanks, be made for all men: for kings and all who are in high places; that we may lead a tranquil and quiet life in all godliness and reverence. For this is good and acceptable in the sight of God our Savior; who desires all people to be saved and come to full knowledge of the truth." (1 Timothy 2:1-4, WEB)

Please pray for Yahweh God Almighty to help:

  • Ultra-Ability Ministry to teach and preach the Word of God with truthfulness, accuracy, love, boldness, and patience
  • The seeds of His Word to germinate in the hearts of people and bring forth many fruits for His praise and glory
  • All people to come to a saving faith and knowledge of Him and His Son, Jesus Christ
  • All people to reverence Him, walk in His ways, serve Him, and keep His commandments as expressions of their true love for Him

Please pray in the name of Jesus Christ, for, as Jesus said, "Most certainly I tell you, whatever you may ask of the Father in my name, he will give it to you." (John 16:23, WEB)


DONATIONS

"Remember this: he who sows sparingly will also reap sparingly. He who sows bountifully will also reap bountifully. Let each man give according as he has determined in his heart; not grudgingly, or under compulsion; for God loves a cheerful giver. And God is able to make all grace abound to you, that you, always having all sufficiency in everything, may abound to every good work." (2 Corinthians 9:6-8, WEB)

Please donate to support Ultra-Ability Ministry in whatever amount your heart desires to give.




Thank you for your prayers and financial support!
